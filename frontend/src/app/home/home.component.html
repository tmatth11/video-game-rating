Welcome to the Video Game Ratings Application

This is a simple application that allows you to review and rate video games. To do this, you must be a registered user. You can also created new games and edit your existing ones by being a registered administrator.

Administrator Abilities

As an administrator, you can create new games, edit your exisitng games, and delete your games. You can also view all the games and reviews that have been created by all users.

User Abilities

As a user, you can view all the games and reviews that have been created by all users. You can post your own reviews along with a rating on a scale of 1 to 10 for each game. These reviews can be edited or deleted by you as well.
